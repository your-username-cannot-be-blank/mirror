I created an open encyclopedia to examine Internet censorship, which is currently only in Persian.

wikicensorship.github.io

ویکی سانسور

دانشنامه ای آزاد برای بررسی سانسور اینترنت

So far, it includes explanations:

  1. about OONI Probes, how to use it, the meanings of the results and how mining in the results.

  2. About DNS and censorship methods in them and how to examine them.

  3. About Network and Transport Layers, examine with ping, traceroute and similar tools. And their strengths and weaknesses.

  4. About Application Layer, examine HTTP and HTTPS and simple methods of censorship in these protocols and the existence of transparent-proxy in port 80. Also about recognizing the difference between network censorship and US sanctions imposed on sites

I am trying to complete the main topics and it will be translated into other languages soon.
