I saw some reports that the domain raw.githubusercontent.com (but not github.com) is blocked in China since about October 30.

  • https://en.greatfire.org/https/raw.githubusercontent.com (archive)

raw.githubusercontent.com is (or was) used for downloads of e.g. prebuilt binaries from GitHub. It probably affects the distribution of a number of circumvention systems.

  • https://twitter.com/adamfisk/status/1190853422787907584 (archive)

    Wow the GFW just blocked all of raw.githubusercontent.com …that’s all of specifically “raw dot githubusercontent dot com” not all of GitHub – aka a smart way to block binary distributions of Lantern installers on GitHub, likely amongst others.
