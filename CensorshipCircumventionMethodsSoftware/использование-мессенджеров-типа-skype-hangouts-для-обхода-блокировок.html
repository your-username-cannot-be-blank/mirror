I don’t know of any ready-to-use systems, but here is a list of research.

SkypeMorph (Code Talker Tunnel) doesn’t actually modulate a signal into the audio. It just imitates some external characteristics of Skype calls (as discussed in The Parrot is Dead).

  • SkypeMorph: Protocol Obfuscation for Tor Bridges (2012)
  • I want my voice to be heard: IP over Voice-over-IP for unobservable censorship circumvention (FreeWave, 2013)
  • SkypeLine: Robust Hidden Data Transmission for VoIP (2016)
  • CovertCast: Using Live Streaming to Evade Internet Censorship (2016)
  • DeltaShaper: Enabling Unobservable Censorship-resistant TCP Tunneling over Videoconferencing Streams (2017)
  • Stegozoa: Enhancing WebRTC Covert Channels with Video Steganography for Internet Censorship Circumvention (2022)
