Some resources on proxying over QUIC or HTTP/3:

  • https://ietf-wg-masque.github.io/
  • Web Censorship Measurements of HTTP/3 over QUIC (IMC 2021) · Issue #113 · net4people/bbs · GitHub

Specific research question: what is the impact of TLS fingerprinting on QUIC? We have https://tlsfingerprint.io/ and tools like JA3 for TLS with TCP, but the fingerprints of TLS in QUIC connections is not studied, as far as I know. Sooner or later, we will need an equivalent of uTLS for QUIC connections.
